100+ Free Online Courses on Mindfulness & Happiness

Learn about mindfulness, happiness, meditation and stress management via this selection of free courses.

Manoel Cortes Mendez Jun 7th, 2022
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 0

The world can be a stressful place. Whether due to the grind of studying, pressure at work, personal relationships, or to large-scale problems — pandemic, global conflicts, societal issues — stress and anxiety are often present in our lives. But mindfulness can help alleviate them.

In his excellent course A Life of Happiness and Fulfillment (which is also included in the list below), Dr. Raj Raghunathan explains that mindfulness involves being in the moment, focusing on what’s happening right now. That doesn’t sound like much, but research has shown that being mindful can help you feel happier and able to handle negative situations with more aplomb.

So mindfulness is closely connected to happiness, and to practices such as meditation and yoga, which can also help reduce stress and anxiety. But true mindfulness is a skill, and it doesn’t come naturally to many people.

Fortunately, there are many resources on the subject available online. Below, you’ll find our selection of free online courses about mindfulness and happiness.

From now on, let us reflect on ourselves and look at our minds leading up to today! Don’t we achieve our goals after knowing ourselves?
